# Privacy Policy for LuminaCal

**Last Updated: December 30, 2024**

## Introduction

LuminaCal ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our calorie tracking application.

## Data Collection

### Data We DO NOT Collect

LuminaCal is designed with privacy-first principles:

- ‚ùå We do **NOT** collect personal information
- ‚ùå We do **NOT** upload your data to any servers
- ‚ùå We do **NOT** require account registration
- ‚ùå We do **NOT** share your data with third parties
- ‚ùå We do **NOT** use analytics or tracking SDKs

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your device** using secure local storage:

- **Meal logs**: Food names, calories, macros, and timestamps
- **Water intake**: Daily water consumption records
- **Weight history**: Weight entries and notes
- **Health metrics**: Age, gender, height, weight, activity level, and fitness goals
- **App preferences**: Dark mode setting, notification preferences

## Camera Usage

LuminaCal uses your device camera **only** for:

- AI-powered food recognition using on-device ML Kit processing
- Image processing happens **entirely on your device**
- Camera images are **NOT stored, saved, or uploaded anywhere**
- Camera data is immediately discarded after recognition

## Data Export

You can export your data at any time:

- **CSV Export**: Export meal logs as spreadsheet
- **JSON Export**: Full backup including all settings

Exported files are saved to your device and are under your control.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Clear All Data
2. Confirm deletion

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

## Children's Privacy

LuminaCal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
